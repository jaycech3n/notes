Encoding CwFs in Homotopy Type Theory

15 Oct 2020

Consider the problem of defining models of type theory inside HoTT. A bit of work has been done on this:

  • Shallow embedding of type theory is morally correct [KKK18]
  • Internal ∞-Categorical Models of Dependent Type Theory: Towards 2LTT Eating HoTT [Kr20]

It seems natural to consider two particular varieties of CwF internal to type theory: the set-truncated and “\infty-” (i.e. with all higher coherences) versions. For the references listed above, the first is formulated in an extensional metatheory and is automatically truncated, while the second is \infty-.

For this note we’ll focus on the set-truncated version.
